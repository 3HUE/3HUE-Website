Enterprise-grade advisory and managed services.

Referral Model

Transparent referral economics and payout timing.

Commission payout

Referral partners receive 5% of total contract value, paid within 30 days after 3HUE receives customer payment.

Duration & cap

Payouts are for 1 year only (projects within a year of acquisition) and are capped at $50K ($1M total contract value). Larger payouts require ICB approval.

Rate governance

Standard rates and the price list are governed by Appendix A. 3HUE may modify rates with 30 days' written notice.
