Who the Referral Partner is for

Partners who introduce qualified opportunities to 3HUE.

The Referral Partner Agreement is designed for organizations that can introduce prospective customers to 3HUE professional services and managed programs.

  • Referrals must be new to 3HUE.
  • Introductions are made in writing or by phone.
  • Qualified referrals must enter a paid services contract within three months.
Relationship Model
  • Referral partners are independent contractors.
  • No authority is granted to bind 3HUE or make commitments on its behalf.
  • 3HUE retains sole discretion to accept or reject referrals.
  • NDA agreements are required.
Rules, Quoting, Help

Clear ownership before, during, and after the sale.

Pre-sale

  • Partner logs into the Partner Portal.
  • Partner registers the deal.
  • 3HUE Client Success team reaches out for a warm hand-off.
  • 3HUE team takes lead from there.

During sale

  • 3HUE owns the selling process.
  • 3HUE controls quoting, discounting, and contracting.

Post-sale

  • 3HUE owns customer relationship for solution delivery.
  • Partner receives reports on compensation.
Commissions & Payouts

Referral payouts and guardrails.

Commission rate

Payouts for referrals are 5% of deal value.

Timing

Payouts occur net 30 after payments are received from the customer.

Duration & cap

Payouts are for 1 year only (projects within a year of acquisition) and are capped at $50K ($1M total contract value). Larger payouts are subject to ICB approval.

No-payment scenario

No commissions are payable if the customer fails to pay 3HUE.

Monthly reporting

Reports are issued within 10 business days after month end and include closed deals, values, payment terms, and commission calculations.

Partner Onboarding

How to get started.

Application process

  • Fill out the online application form on the 3HUE website.
  • Get a Partner ID and access to the Partner Portal immediately.
  • Register a deal (if applicable).

Onboarding & training

  • Partners can undergo onboarding to understand program details and benefits.
  • Training sessions are available to promote and leverage 3HUE services.

Ongoing support

  • Dedicated partner support team available for assistance.
  • Regular updates and resources to help partners succeed.
Agreement Terms

Term, termination, and confidentiality requirements.

  • Agreement term is one year with automatic renewal unless 30 days' notice is provided.
  • Termination for cause requires 30 days' written notice and a cure period.
  • Final report is delivered within 30 days after termination; commissions continue for qualified referrals closed prior to termination.
  • Confidentiality obligations survive termination for five years.
Compliance, Indemnity & Territory
  • Referral activities must comply with applicable laws and protect 3HUE's reputation.
  • Each party indemnifies the other for claims arising from its own acts, omissions, or breaches.
  • Territory is defined as the United States and described as North America in the agreement; 3HUE may modify territory with 30 days' notice.
