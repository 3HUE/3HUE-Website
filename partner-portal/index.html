Program Overview

Governed referrals with clear qualification, reporting, and compensation.

3HUE Executive Consulting, LLC provides professional services and managed programs. The referral program formalizes how partners introduce prospective customers and how qualified referrals are tracked and compensated.

  • Qualified referrals must be new to 3HUE, introduced in writing or by phone, and contract within three months.
  • Referrals are submitted through the partner portal referral form and confirmed in writing if accepted.
  • 3HUE retains sole discretion to accept or reject any referral.
  • Partners apply for a unique Partner ID to track deals and payments.
Relationship & Governance
  • Partners operate as independent contractors with no authority to bind 3HUE.
  • Confidential information is protected and survives for five years after termination.
  • Referral activities must comply with applicable laws and avoid misleading representations.
  • Governing law: Florida. Disputes are resolved via binding arbitration in Broward County, Florida.

Benefits & Economics

Transparent referral economics and rate governance.

Commission payout

Referral partners receive 5% of total contract value, paid within 30 days after 3HUE receives customer payment.

No-payment scenario

No commissions are payable if the customer fails to pay 3HUE.

Rate governance

Standard rates and the price list are governed by the applicable rate table. 3HUE may modify rates with 30 days' written notice.

Reporting & Term

Monthly reporting with clear term structure.

  • Monthly reports are delivered within 10 business days after month end.
  • Reports include closed deals, deal values, payment terms, and commission calculations.
  • Agreement term is one year and renews automatically unless 30 days' notice is provided.
  • Termination for cause requires 30 days' written notice and a cure period.
  • Final report is delivered within 30 days after termination and commissions continue for qualified referrals closed prior to termination.
Territory
  • Territory is defined as the United States in the agreement definitions.
  • The territory scope section describes North America.
  • 3HUE may modify territory with 30 days' notice.
Services Overview

Services referenced in the referral rate table.

Prospecting & introductory services

The program includes discovery-aligned services such as CISO Scan, introductory advisory retainers, and secure engineering & architecture retainers.

Risk posture assessments

Rate tables include multiple frameworks (HIPAA, CMMC-L2, CIS-IG2, SOC 2, CSF 2.0, C2M2, PCI-DSS, NIST-MOD).

Programs & managed services

Services include vCISO, ISP, RMP, VCP, CIRP, SCS, and the ISG managed security operations service catalog.

How to Engage

Structured steps from application to payout.

1. Apply for a Partner ID

Complete the application in the partner portal to receive a unique Partner ID for tracking.

2. Submit a referral

Provide a written or phone introduction and submit the referral through the portal form.

3. Confirm, report, and pay

3HUE confirms acceptance in writing, reports monthly, and issues commission payments per the agreement.
